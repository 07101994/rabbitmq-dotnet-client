Remarks

In this method, we assume that the stream that backs our NetworkBinaryWriter is a positionable stream - which it is currently (see Frame.m_accumulator, Frame.GetWriter and Command.Transmit).

Supports the AMQP 0-8/0-9 standard entry types S, I, D, T and F, as well as the QPid-0-8 specific b, d, f, l, s, t x and V types and the AMQP 0-9-1 A type.
